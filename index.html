Hi! I'm Ryan and I'm a B.F.A. Graphic Design student at Trinity Christian College in Palos Heights, IL! I am originally from Oskaloosa, IA; where I grew up on a hog farm. I like shrimp, shopping, art, monochrome white Converse, and the Iowa Hawkeyes. I don't like mashed potatoes.


A project I have been working on recently has led to some interesting discoveries. I have been traveling around eastern Iowa exploring ghost towns. In these towns I am documenting a green velvet chair and I will be creating a publication displaying the images. I hope to use this project to bring awareness to Rural Flight and the causes and effects of said trend.
